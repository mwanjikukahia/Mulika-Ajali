Our Road Safety Principles

Our mission is guided by core principles that promote safety and responsibility on the road:

  • Responsibility
  • Alertness
  • Respect for Laws
  • Care for Pedestrians
  • Defensive Driving
  • Vehicle Maintenance
  • Environmental Protection

Road Safety Campaigns

We organize national campaigns such as the “Mulika Ajali, Okoa Maisha” initiative, which spreads awareness about the importance of road safety through media, events, and community engagement.

Public Transport Training

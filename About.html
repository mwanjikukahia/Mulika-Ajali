Who We Are

Mulika Ajali is dedicated to improving road safety through comprehensive awareness campaigns, public training, and advocacy. Our mission is to create safer roads for everyone by equipping the public with essential safety knowledge and supporting policy initiatives that enhance safety standards across Kenya.

With a presence across several regions, we aim to educate, empower, and bring impactful changes to reduce road accidents. Join us as we work towards a safer and more secure future on the roads.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce road accidents in Kenya
by raising awareness, improving roadsafety practices, and advocating for the wellness of public transportworkers.
We aim to create a safer transport environment for all by focusingon education,
collaboration, and long-term behavioral changes.

Our Objectives

Raise Road Safety Awareness

Enhancing road safety knowledge within communities to prevent accidents and promote a culture of responsible road use.

Empower Public Transport Workers

Providing educational resources and training programs to equip transport workers with skills for safe and efficient service delivery.

Wellness Programs

Our wellness programs focus on their physical and mental health. We offer workshops that teach them how to manage stress, maintain good health, and balance work with their personal lives.

Foster Community Partnerships

Collaborating with local and national organizations to implement impactful road safety initiatives.

Promote Road Safety Innovation

Encouraging the development of new tools, strategies, and technologies that improve road safety outcomes.
